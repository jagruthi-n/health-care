Items You Can Easily Consume In Obesity Diet Plan Choose minimally processed, whole foods:

  • Steamed snacks You can steam carrots and beans and mix them with some salt, paprika and pepper for a quick and tasty snack. There are also many Indian foods like dhokla and khandvi made by steaming a batter made of chickpeas. They are also very healthy and satisfying.

  • chaat Boil a cup of mixed sprouts and toss them with some chaat powder, chopped tomatoes, onions and coriander leaves.

  • Baked snacks Slice potatoes, carrots or sweet potatoes into thick wedges, sprinkle them with some salt and pepper and roast them in the oven until they are fully cooked.
